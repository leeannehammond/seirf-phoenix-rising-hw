Instructions

Welcome to Landscaper! So, you want to start a landscaping business? Use your tools to earn more money!

You win the game when you have a team of starving students helping and $1,000.

To start, the only tool you have are your teeth. Using just tool, you can spend the day cutting lawns and make money. You can do this as much as you want.

After $5, you can buy a pair of scissors.

Once you have $25, you can buy an old-timey lawnmower and make $50.

Want a battery-powered lawnmower? Buy it for $250 and make $100.

Hire a team of starving students for $500 and make $250.

Win the game once you've made $1000.
